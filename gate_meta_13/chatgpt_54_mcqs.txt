We can use the formula for modulus of Burgers' vector- 

$|\vec{b}| = \frac{\mu b}{2\pi a}$

where $\mu$ is the shear modulus, $a$ is the atomic diameter and $b$ is the Burgers' vector.

Substituting the given values, we get

$b = \frac{2\pi a|\vec{b}|}{\mu} = \frac{2\pi \times 0.25 \ nm \times |\vec{b}|}{70 \ GPa}$

Converting GPa to nm$^{-2}$, we get

$b = \frac{2\pi \times 0.25 \times |\vec{b}|}{70 \times 10^3}$

$b = 0.00225 \times |\vec{b}|$

Comparing with the options given, we see that the correct answer is:

(B) 0.25 nm.
