$i_a = i_0 * 10^{\frac{(V_a - V_0)}{b}}$

$V_a$ = Anodic polarization (V)

$i_0 = 10^{-5}$ A m^(-2) (Exchange current density)

$i_a = 10^{-5} * 10^{\frac{0.4}{0.1}}$
