(P) Blast furnace runner - used for smelting iron ore and producing pig iron which can then be further processed in other refineries.

(R) Torpedo car - used for transporting hot metal (molten iron) from blast furnace to the BOF converter.

(S) BOF (Basic Oxygen Furnace) - used for refining the hot metal from blast furnace with pure oxygen, to remove impurities such as carbon, silicon, and phosphorus.

(Q) AOD (Argon Oxygen Decarburization) - used for refining stainless steel through the removal of carbon and other impurities.

Therefore, the matching is:
(A) P-4, Q-1, R-2, S-3
