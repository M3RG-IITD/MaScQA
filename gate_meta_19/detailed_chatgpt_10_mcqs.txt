The common ore of titanium is Ilmenite.

Explanation:
Titanium is mainly obtained from ilmenite, which is a type of titanium-iron oxide mineral. Ilmenite contains about 45-60% titanium and varying amounts of iron, aluminum and magnesium oxides. It is the most important ore of titanium and is typically mined in Australia, Canada, India, Norway, South Africa, Ukraine, and the United States. 

Therefore, the correct answer is (D) Ilmenite.
