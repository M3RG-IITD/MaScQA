As the integral is equal to 4, we have:

$\int_{0}^{2} bx(b-x) dx = 4$

Expanding the integrand we get:

$\int_{0}^{2} (b x^2 - b x^3) dx = 4$

Integrating term by term, we get:

$\frac{b}{3} x^3 - \frac{b}{4} x^4 \Big|_0^2 = 4$

Substituting x=2 and x=0:

$\frac{b}{3} (2^3) - \frac{b}{4} (2^4) - \frac{b}{3} (0^3) + \frac{b}{4} (0^4) = 4$

Simplifying:

$\frac{8b}{3} - 4b = 4$

$\frac{8b-12b}{3} = -4$

$-\frac{4b}{3} = -4$

$b = \frac{3*4}{4} = \frac{3}{1} = 3$

Therefore, the value of the constant b is 3.
