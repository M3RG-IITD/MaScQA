To find the stress amplitude, we'll first need to calculate the mean stress and the stress range. 

1. Calculate the mean stress:

Mean stress (σ_m) = (Maximum stress + Minimum stress) / 2
σ_m = (400 MPa - (-300 MPa)) / 2

Sigma_m represents the mean stress.

2. Calculate the stress range:

Stress range (Δσ) = Maximum stress - Minimum stress
Δσ = 400 MPa - (-300 MPa)

Delta_sigma represents the stress range.

3. Calculate the stress amplitude:

Stress amplitude (σ_a) = Stress range / 2
σ_a = Δσ / 2

Sigma_a represents the stress amplitude.
